伊斯坦布尔美食日记 - Istanbul Food Journal

伊斯坦布尔的美食文化融合了东西方的精华。在香料市场品尝了传统的土耳其软糖和干果，色彩缤纷，味道丰富。午餐在加拉塔桥附近的一家餐厅享用了经典的土耳其烤肉串和皮塔饼，配以新鲜的沙拉和酸奶酱。晚餐在博斯普鲁斯海峡畔的一家传统餐厅品尝了奥斯曼帝国的宫廷料理：烤羊肉、茄子烤肉饼、土耳其米饭和果仁蜜饼。土耳其咖啡和茶文化也令人印象深刻。

Istanbul's culinary culture blends the best of East and West. At the Spice Bazaar, I tasted traditional Turkish delight and dried fruits, colorful and rich in flavor. For lunch at a restaurant near Galata Bridge, I enjoyed classic Turkish kebabs and pita bread with fresh salad and yogurt sauce. For dinner at a traditional restaurant by the Bosphorus, I savored Ottoman imperial cuisine: roasted lamb, eggplant kebab, Turkish rice, and baklava. Turkish coffee and tea culture was also impressive.
