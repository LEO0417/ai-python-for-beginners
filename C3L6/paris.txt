巴黎美食日记 - Paris Food Journal

在巴黎的第一天，我开始了美食探索之旅。早餐在蒙马特高地的一家小咖啡馆享用了新鲜的法式牛角包和咖啡。午餐在拉丁区的一家传统法式餐厅品尝了蜗牛、鹅肝酱和法式洋葱汤。晚餐在塞纳河畔的一家米其林星级餐厅享用了经典的法国料理：法式牛排配红酒汁、松露土豆泥和法式焦糖布丁。每一道菜都展现了法国烹饪的精髓。

On my first day in Paris, I began my culinary exploration. For breakfast at a small café in Montmartre, I enjoyed fresh croissants and coffee. For lunch at a traditional French restaurant in the Latin Quarter, I tasted escargots, foie gras, and French onion soup. For dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant by the Seine, I enjoyed classic French cuisine: steak au poivre with red wine sauce, truffle mashed potatoes, and crème brûlée. Each dish showcased the essence of French cooking.
